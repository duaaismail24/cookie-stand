IVAR'S LOCATIONS AND MENUS

From its early beginnings at Seattle's waterfront in 1938, to today, Ivar’s has made its way from Bellingham to Tacoma, from the Elliott Bay waterfront east to Spokane, and scattered its clams in a few points in between. The Ivar’s dining experience offers 21 fast casual Seafood Bars and three full-service waterfront-view restaurants: Ivar's Acres of Clams, Ivar's Salmon House and Ivar's Mukilteo Landing. Ivar's also operates stadium concessions in Safeco Field, CenturyLink and Cheney Stadium. No matter which restaurant you choose, you’ll find the award-winning Ivar’s seafood you love.

IVAR'S GROUP DINING

Our restaurants are all located in the Seattle area and have 180º views of Elliott Bay, Lake Union or Puget Sound. Those stunning views set the stage for a spectacular private dining event. Ivar's Salmon House can help you make the most of your rehearsal dinner, intimate wedding, business meeting, anniversary party, retirement party, family reunion, or any other private dining function you can think of.

Ivar's offers the very best of the Northwest in our restaurants. With unsurpassed views of Puget Sound our Seattle banquet rooms can accommodate your guest list from 20 to 150. We offer a feast of fresh Northwest seafood and local specialties. Our professional event planners are happy to assist you with your every need and will make your planning fun and easy.
